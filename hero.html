For job seekers: how to ace your interview

Need some advice for the big day? We polled our recruiters to find out what they look for in a successful interview. Here's one of their top tips: "Ask the recruiter what to expect in your interview. They will often have great feedback from previous interviews and know a lot about what qualities the hiring manager is looking for. They've narrowed down their search to find you, and they can help you understand what will best demonstrate to the hiring manager that you're right for the job."
