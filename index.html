For job seekers: how to ace your interview

Need some advice for the big day? We polled our recruiters to find out what they look for in a successful interview. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:
It might seem simple, but don't forget the basics.
  • Be engaged. Look at the person interviewing you, not the floor or the wall above their head.
  • Bring a notebook and take notes of important information. If you're interviewed by multiple people, share some of what their peers have discussed.
  • Arrive a few minutes early: make a great first impression by being on time.
